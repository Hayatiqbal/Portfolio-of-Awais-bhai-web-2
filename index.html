FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What email platforms do you specialize in? +
I specialize in Klaviyo, Omnisend, and Shopify Email. These are the three most powerful platforms for ecommerce email marketing. I can also work with Mailchimp and ActiveCampaign if your business uses those.
How quickly can I expect to see results? +
Most clients see measurable revenue improvements within the first 2–4 weeks once flows are live. The abandoned cart and welcome flows typically show results almost immediately. Full optimization takes 60–90 days of continuous testing.
Do you write the email copy as well? +
Yes! I handle everything end-to-end — strategy, copywriting, design, automation setup, and ongoing optimization. You get a complete, done-for-you email marketing system.
What types of ecommerce businesses do you work with? +
I work with Shopify stores, Amazon sellers, DTC brands, beauty & skincare brands, fashion & apparel, health & wellness, electronics, home goods, and most other ecommerce verticals. The email strategies are tailored to your specific niche.
How do we get started? +
Simply book a free 30-minute strategy call. We'll discuss your current situation, your goals, and I'll share a custom plan for growing your email revenue. No commitment required — just a conversation to see if we're a great fit.
Can you work with an existing Klaviyo/Omnisend account? +
Absolutely. I can audit and optimize your existing setup or build from scratch. Many clients come with existing accounts that need restructuring, and we typically see dramatic improvements from a proper audit and rebuild.
