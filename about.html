My Story

Building Email Systems That Actually Generate Revenue

I'm Muhammad Awais, a Top Rated Ecommerce Email Marketing Specialist on Upwork, and I've spent years developing a deep expertise in one thing: helping ecommerce businesses extract maximum revenue from their email lists.

My journey began when I realized that most ecommerce brands were sitting on an untapped goldmine — their email subscribers. They had massive lists, but were using generic campaigns, broken automations, and no real strategy. I saw an opportunity to change that.

Over the years, I've worked with 50+ Shopify stores, Amazon sellers, and DTC brands across industries — from beauty and skincare to electronics, fashion, health & wellness, and beyond. Each brand taught me something new. Every campaign sharpened my skills. Every data point made my strategies better.

Today, I help ecommerce businesses build complete email marketing ecosystems: from welcome flows that convert browsers into buyers, to post-purchase sequences that turn one-time customers into lifetime advocates. My approach is always revenue-first, data-driven, and built for long-term sustainable growth.
