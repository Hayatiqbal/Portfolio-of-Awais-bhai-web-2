FitGear Sports: From $8K to $31K Monthly Email Revenue in 90 Days

FitGear Sports was a growing Shopify fitness apparel brand with a large email list but virtually no automation. They were sending sporadic newsletter blasts with no segmentation, no welcome flow, and no abandoned cart recovery — leaving massive revenue on the table.

I built a complete Klaviyo ecosystem from scratch: a 6-email welcome series, a 4-email abandoned cart flow with strategic incentives, a browse abandonment sequence, and a post-purchase flow with cross-sell recommendations. Combined with a monthly campaign calendar, email revenue went from $8K to $31K in 90 days — a 287% increase.

NovaTech Electronics: Email Becomes 35% of Total Revenue with Omnisend

NovaTech Electronics had a decent Omnisend setup but it wasn't performing. Open rates were at 18%, click rates under 2%, and email barely contributed 8% to total revenue. The automations were basic, the segmentation was non-existent, and the templates looked dated.

I rebuilt their entire Omnisend infrastructure. New segmentation strategy based on purchase history and browsing behavior, redesigned email templates, 5 new automation flows, and a strategic campaign calendar. Within 6 months, email went from 8% to 35% of total revenue, contributing $48K per month.

LuxeSkin Beauty: Welcome Series Converts at 34% — Better Than Paid Ads

LuxeSkin Beauty had heavy paid ad dependency and a costly customer acquisition problem. Their email list was growing but had almost zero automation. New subscribers received a single welcome email and then nothing until the next promotional blast.

HomeNest Goods: 156% Customer Lifetime Value Increase Through Retention Automation

HomeNest Goods sold through both Amazon and their Shopify store. Their challenge wasn't acquisition — it was retention. Customers bought once and rarely returned. They had no post-purchase automation and no win-back strategy.

I built a comprehensive retention ecosystem: a post-purchase thank you + cross-sell flow, a product education series, a replenishment reminder flow, and a win-back campaign for lapsed customers. The repeat purchase rate grew from 18% to 42%, average order LTV increased 156%, and the win-back flow alone recovers $22K in monthly revenue from previously "lost" customers.

VitalWell Health: Deliverability Overhaul Drives 215% Open Rate Improvement

VitalWell Health had a massive 80,000-subscriber list but was getting 12% open rates and their emails were landing in spam for many users. Their sending reputation was damaged from years of sending to unengaged subscribers.

I performed a complete list hygiene overhaul: removing invalid emails, segmenting the list by engagement levels, setting up a re-engagement campaign, and implementing proper suppression lists. Combined with deliverability best practices and sending reputation rebuilding, open rates went from 12% to 37% — a 215% improvement — and campaign ROI grew 3.1x.
